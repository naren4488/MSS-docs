## 🔴 CRITICAL GAPS (Must Address)

### 1. **Performance/Savings Guarantee** - BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY
**What SolarSquare Does:**
- GoodZero promise: Compensates ₹8 per unit if generation is below committed
- Example: If guaranteed 4,200 units in Year 1, but only got 3,000, customer gets ₹9,600
- Creates trust and differentiates from competitors
- Shows confidence in system quality

**Current MSS Status:** ❌ No performance guarantee
**Impact:** High - This is why customers choose SolarSquare over Craft
**Suggestion:** Create a "Performance Guarantee" for Bhanu and other clients
- Guaranteed generation based on location/sun hours
- Clear compensation mechanism (₹X per unit shortfall)
- Builds massive customer trust

---

### 2. **EMI/Flexible Financing** - CUSTOMER CONVERSION BLOCKER
**What SolarSquare Does:**
```
6 Months:   ₹35,233/month
36 Months:  ₹6,941/month  (Most popular)
60 Months:  ₹4,702/month
Down payment: ₹90,600
```
- Removes payment barrier
- 3rd party NBFC partnerships
- Clear terms & conditions

**Current MSS Status:** ❌ Only 30-60-10 cash payment
**Impact:** CRITICAL - Many customers can't afford ₹3-5L upfront
**Suggestion:** Partner with NBFC to offer 24, 36, 48, 60-month EMI options
- Increases customer base significantly
- Shown in quotation as alternative to cash

---

### 3. **Detailed Maintenance & Service Plan** - CUSTOMER RETENTION
**What SolarSquare Does:**
- **PowerBoost visits**: Minimum 45 visits/year
- Proactive cleaning, monitoring, repairs
- Customer provides: Ladder, water, electricity, WiFi, access
- Clear responsibility matrix

**Current MSS Status:** ⚠️ Mentions "2 year FREE maintenance" but no detail
**Impact:** Medium - Affects post-installation satisfaction
**Suggestion:** Add detailed maintenance schedule to quotation
- Quarterly/bi-annual cleaning
- Annual inspection checklist
- Response time for issues (e.g., 24-48 hours)
- What's included vs what's paid after warranty

## QUESTIONS FOR YOU (BEFORE IMPLEMENTATION)

1. **Performance Guarantee:**
   - Are you comfortable guaranteeing generation figures?
   - What's your actual generation uncertainty? (±5%? ±10%?)
   - At what compensation level? (₹5/unit? ₹8/unit?)

2. **EMI Financing:**
   - Do you have an NBFC partner ready?
   - What tenor would you offer? (24/36/48/60 months?)
   - Who handles default risk?

3. **Maintenance Plan:**
   - How many visits per year can you do?
   - What's the service area (Jaipur city only? Rajasthan?)
   - What's the cost after 2-year free period?

4. **Insurance Coverage:**
   - Would you offer water leakage coverage? Cost?
   - Anti-cyclone? (Already covered by structure?)
   - Zero repair cost? For how long? Cost?

5. **Priority:**
   - Which 3 features are MOST important for you to add?
   - Which would help you win more customers?
   - Which align with your strengths?
